INTERNATIONAL WOMEN'S DAY

WHERE'S THE GAP?

Today is International Women’s Day. We’re here to celebrate not only the accomplishments of women, but the work they are doing in improving the climate of women everywhere. They are taking steps to reduce the gender gap in various industries and are pushing the needle towards change.

But, where exactly is the gap?

This visualization aims to shed light on the various industries and areas where there is a large gap between men and women. But we’re not just talking about that. We also highlight the women who are making these gaps a little smaller. Happy International Women’s Day.




Maya Frai, March 8 2019
