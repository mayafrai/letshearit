ABOUT

Women are doing incredible things in various industries. The mission of LHI Industry is to highlight leading women in industries like e-commerce, mobility, fintech, and more as well as educate the community on the latest trends and advancements that women are contributing to. Stay updated on new industry interviews by subscribing to the LHI Newsletter.

Dagne Dover: Deepa Gandhi, Melissa Mash, Jessy Dover

June 25, 2019

Female founders are leading the fashion industry towards a new movement in quality production and customer acquisition. The industry is no longer for those who can produce the most, but for those who can capitalize on what consumers really need. Quality over quantity is a major proponent of Dagne Dover's value proposition and their dedication to this mission is commendable. Read more to learn about how they get it done.
